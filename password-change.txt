Password Change
Overview
The password change feature allows users to securely update their account passwords. This feature ensures that users can maintain the security of their accounts by regularly updating their passwords.

Steps to Change Password
Log In to Your Account:

Navigate to the login page.
Enter your username and current password.
Click the "Log In" button.
Access Account Settings:

Once logged in, go to the account settings page by clicking on your profile icon in the top-right corner.
Select "Settings" from the dropdown menu.

Fill all your details in the profile page.
